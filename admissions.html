Admissions at Voorpos Primary School

We welcome applications from families who wish to provide their children with a nurturing and supportive learning environment. Our admissions process is designed to be simple, transparent, and accessible to all prospective parents.

At Voorpos Primary School, we are committed to helping every learner reach their full potential through quality education, dedicated educators, and a strong partnership with parents.

Required Documents

To ensure a smooth application process, please prepare and submit the following documents when applying for admission:

Birth Certificate and ID photo

Certified copy of the learner’s birth certificate and an A6 photo of the child.

Latest School Report

Most recent academic report from previous school.

Parent/Guardian ID

Copy of ID document for parent or guardian.

Proof of Address

Recent utility bill or official document.

Medical Information

Any important medical details about the learner.
