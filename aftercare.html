Aftercare Program

Providing a safe, supportive environment for learners after school hours

About Our Aftercare

Our Aftercare program is designed to support working parents by providing a safe, structured, and caring environment for learners after school. We focus on homework supervision, recreational activities, and personal development.

Our dedicated staff create a nurturing atmosphere where every child feels valued, supported, and encouraged to grow in confidence. We aim to build not only strong learners, but well-rounded individuals who are prepared for future challenges.

Operating Hours

Monday – Friday 13:00 – 17:30
Closed on public holidays and school holidays
